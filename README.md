## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a **Semester 6 Information Systems student** at **Batam Institute of Technology (ITEBA)** with a **3.79 GPA**, currently specializing in **Machine Learning Operations** at **Infinite Learning**.

My expertise spans across **Backend Development**, **Full-Stack Web Development**, and **Generative AI**. I have hands-on experience building production-ready applications—from real-time logistics systems to AI-powered medical screening tools using deep learning.

Previously worked as a **Generative AI Developer at Dicoding Indonesia** and completed a **Web Developer Internship at PT Otak Kanan**, where I honed my skills in building scalable web applications and integrating AI capabilities.
